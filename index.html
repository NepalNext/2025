About Us

What is NepalNext™?

NepalNext™ is a digital youth-led project where young Nepalis around the world, aged 13-28, can collaborate to address problems, share ideas, policies, and innovations for Nepal's future.

Submitters can express their ideas by submitting essays, videos, designs, slideshows, and other forms of content. The best youth-created submissions each year will be brought together and published into an annual NepalNext™ Youth Manifesto at the end of the Gregorian calendar year.

This manifesto will be open to the public and delivered to schools, community leaders, media outlets, and policymakers to help lead to real action.

Our Mission
NepalNext™ allows young Nepalis worldwide to collaborate, create, and share innovative ideas that shape a better future for Nepal.

NepalNext™ values creativity, collaboration, transparency, and sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can submit to NepalNext™?

We welcome submissions from creative youth aged 13-28 years old from Nepal and around the world who are passionate about innovation and positive change.

What types of submissions do you accept?

We accept submissions such as essays, art, poetry, slideshows, videos, and any other type of innovative solution that covers one's topic. Submissions can either be in English or Nepali and may be translated into each other's language for clarity.

When will the youth manifesto be published?

The NepalNext™ Youth Manifesto is published annually at the end or start of the Gregorian calendar year. Follow our social media for updates on important dates.

What happens after I submit?

Our team reviews all submissions. Selected works may be featured on our website, Instagram, or included in our annual manifesto publication. You will be notified if you have been selected by email.
